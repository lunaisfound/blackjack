Player's Options

  • Hit 🎯 – You request another card to try to improve your hand. Be careful, as going over 21 means you bust and lose!
  • Stand ✋ – You keep your current hand and end your turn, hoping it’s strong enough to beat the dealer.
  • Split ✂️ – If you have two cards of the same rank, you can split them into two separate hands, doubling your bet and playing each hand individually.
